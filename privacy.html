Privacy Policy

Detour Buddy is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly. This page explains what the app stores on your device, what it does not collect, and how optional features (like subscriptions) work.

At a glance

No account required No social features No ads No tracking Local saved list (Pro)

Detour Buddy uses your location (while the app is in use) to search for nearby places and show you quick detour options. By default, we do not collect your precise location, we do not sell your data, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Important: Place results come from Apple MapKit search and may vary by region. Detour Buddy does not control the underlying place database.

Effective date: January 12, 2026

Data stored on your device

Detour Buddy is designed to work without an account. The following data may be stored locally on your device:

  • Saved places (Pro): places you save, including name, approximate coordinates, and optional note text.
  • App preferences: selected vibe, time budget, quality mode selection, and other UI settings.
  • Basic app state: things like “last selected tab” or whether an info banner is shown.

Device permissions

  • Location (While Using the App): Used to search for nearby detours. Detour Buddy requests location access only through iOS system permission prompts.

Detour Buddy does not access your contacts, photos library, microphone, or camera.

Data we collect

Precise location: Detour Buddy uses your location on-device to perform “near me” searches. We do not collect, store, or upload your precise location to our servers.

Saved notes: Notes you type for saved places are stored on your device. We do not collect or read those notes.

Tracking: Detour Buddy does not use advertising identifiers and does not track you across apps or websites.

Analytics: By default, Detour Buddy does not include third-party analytics SDKs. If analytics are added in the future, we will update this policy and provide an in-app option where applicable.

Optional subscription status

If you subscribe to Detour Buddy Pro, subscription transactions are processed by Apple. Detour Buddy does not receive your payment details. The app may check your purchase entitlement using StoreKit to determine whether Pro features are enabled.

Third-party services

Apple services

  • MapKit / Local Search: Used to search for nearby places and open directions in Apple Maps.
  • Apple Maps: When you tap Maps, the app hands off to Apple Maps for directions.
  • In-App Purchases / Subscriptions (Pro): Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. Detour Buddy does not receive your payment card information.

Other third parties

Detour Buddy does not include advertising networks and does not share your saved places or notes with third parties.

Your choices and controls

Saved data

  • You can delete saved places inside the app (Pro).
  • You can remove the app to delete local data stored on your device (subject to iOS behavior and backups).

Location permissions

  • You can manage location access in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.
  • If you deny location access, Detour Buddy cannot provide “near me” detours.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. You can view, change, or cancel your subscription in: iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Detour Buddy does not sell data. If you contact support, we’ll only use your email to respond to your request.

Children’s privacy

Detour Buddy is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through support contact, please email us and we will address it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. When we update it, we will revise the “Effective date” above.
