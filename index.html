What Detour Buddy does

Detour Buddy finds things worth stopping for near your current location. It’s designed for real life: you’re already out, you have a little time, and you want a quick win.

  • Near-me search with driving or walking modes.
  • Time budget controls to find realistic detours.
  • ETA estimates refined with Apple Maps for top results.
  • Curated filters to reduce obvious chains and low-signal results.

No accounts. No feeds. No “plan a whole day.” Just fast, nearby discovery.

Core experience

  • Pick a vibe: Scenic, Coffee, Quirky, Bars, Restaurants (availability depends on Pro).
  • Choose a time budget: e.g., 10 / 15 / 30 minutes.
  • Get a clean list: Detour Buddy ranks nearby options with short “why” lines.
  • Open instantly: One tap launches Apple Maps for directions.
  • Optional research: Open a place in Safari or run a quick web search.
  • Driving or walking: Pick your mode for more realistic results.
  • ETA-aware results: Detours are filtered to fit your time budget.
  • Smart ETA estimates: We estimate ETAs for all results and refine the top picks using Apple Maps.

Why the results feel curated

Detour Buddy doesn’t just dump map results. It applies lightweight heuristics to reduce “meh” results and keep the list useful.

  • Variety: Avoids flooding the list with the same type of place.
  • Quality signals: For food/drink, downranks obvious chains and low-signal retail.
  • Two modes: “Anything” is broad; “Best” is stricter and can relax filters when results are sparse (Pro).

Quick Start

  1. Open Detour Buddy and allow Location while using the app.
  2. Choose your vibe (Scenic is always available).
  3. Choose driving or walking.
  4. Set your time budget (how far you’re willing to go).
  5. Tap Find detours.
  6. ETAs are estimated immediately and refined for top results.
  7. Tap Maps for directions, or Web/Search to learn more.
Pro tip: Use Scenic for quick “reset” stops — viewpoints, parks, overlooks — the kind of detour that feels like you got more out of the day.

Free vs Pro

Detour Buddy is free for quick scenic detours. Pro unlocks additional vibes, saving, and higher-quality curation controls.

  • Free: Scenic vibe, “Anything” quality mode, open in Maps / Web / Search.
  • Pro: All vibes, “Best” quality mode, save places and add notes.

Important: In the free version, other vibes are visible but disabled. Saving is also disabled (buttons remain visible but won’t activate).

What Pro includes

  • More vibes: Coffee, Quirky, Bars, Restaurants, Parks, EV Charging, Rest Areas, and more.
  • Best mode: Stricter filtering + better ranking for higher-quality picks.
  • Saved places: Bookmark detours you want to revisit.
  • Notes: Add quick notes (with undo + counter) so you remember why you saved it.
